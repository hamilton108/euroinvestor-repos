Company profile

Statoil (STL) is an energy company primarily focused on upstream oil and gas. STL has operations worldwide, yet major operations are on the Norwegian Continental Shelf (NCS). As the largest offshore operator in the world, STL’s main focus is deep and harsh environment. Besides production risk, the oil price is the most important factor for STL, influencing both earnings and the willingness to invest in new projects.
